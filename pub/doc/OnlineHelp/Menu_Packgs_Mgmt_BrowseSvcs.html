Browse Services Screen
Screen Heading: Packages > Management > packageName > Services
Use the Browse Services screen to view the list of services and specifications in a package.
To open the Browse Services screen for a package, go to the Packages Management Screen and click the package name. The Package Description Screen will be displayed. Click Browse services in packageName.
Click Return to properties for packageName to return to the previous screen.
Services
Lists the fully qualified names of the services and specifications in the package. To view detailed information about a resource, click its name.
