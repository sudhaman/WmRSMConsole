Messaging Screen
Screen Heading: Settings > Messaging
Use the Messaging screen to access screens for setting up and managing webMethods messaging triggers and JMS triggers.
To open this screen, click Messaging under the Settings menu on the Navigation panel.
Under webMethods Messaging Configuration:
Click webMethods Messaging Settings to view messaging connection aliases created for the webMethods Broker and webMethods Universal Messaging.
Click webMethods Messaging Trigger Management to manage webMethods messaging triggers.
Under JMS Configuration:
Click JMS Settings to view JMS connection aliases.
Click JMS Trigger Management to manage JMS triggers.
Click JNDI Settings to view JNDI provider aliases.
